1.       The team has completed automation for the whole Regression suite and new user stories for UI validation and end to end feature testing. We discussed and created a new OQ package for FlexRandomizer 3.0.2 because it was hard and time-consuming to convert the existing OQ package in the new format as it is. So overall, we have created around 560 test cases with different scenarios out of which 264 are automated and successfully executed. For randomness testing, 147 test cases executed successfully and validated against the benchmark. The team took many efforts for validating previously created benchmarks as we have found a couple of bugs that were difficult to reproduce. But with the team's positive & dedicated approach we fixed it & created new benchmarks. To validate the results, we have added an R validator for Kit, even though it was not in the scope.

2.        The team performed excellently even with varying team size- with initial team size being 12 to getting reduced to 7 (currently) and working without an Independent tester was a great achievement. The team faced a lot of impediments due to these unexpected changes but never gave up and managed each role efficiently. The team is consistently on track even though the team size is reducing and scope is increasing at the same time.

3.       While using the new Automation Testing tool team faced many technical challenges. From time to time, team raised support queries with Ranorex. The team worked to find out the solution for the same and restructured the automation test scripts and created multiple Ranorex projects efficiently.

4.       The team has taken really good efforts on automated unit testing even though it increased our scope. With the current architecture, we are implementing automated unit testing. The team found a new tool for code coverage. As this is legacy product and code is entangled so the team found a mechanism to mock the sessions.

5.       Due to effective testing by Team, we have addressed many ongoing and legacy bugs reported by the team. It added value to our product.

6.       Even though many team members were not part of Agile liftoff sessions, the team followed the Agile approach with a strong and positive mindset. As a team we helped each other to boost trust by following simple rules like Openness, honest and transparent communication. Strong intent to work in Generalizing specialist manner and team performed accordingly.

7.       To enhance customer satisfaction, the team took meetings with Michael and Mindy. In those meetings, the focus was on how they use our product. As an end-user point of view, the team discussed & added new features like sorting, searching and page length on dashboards for better user experience.

8.       The team followed all the processes, due to which we have successfully faced a surprised Bayer audit on ongoing projects. The team fixed important issues of the Bayer audit. The team has incorporated all the important issues of Bayer audit in the current release with a positive approach even if it was not planned earlier in the scope.
